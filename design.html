Responsive Design.

Google recommends webmasters follow the industry best practice of using responsive web design, namely serving the same HTML for all devices and using only CSS media queries to decide the rendering on each device.

What does this mean?  The latest buzzword around town is responsive design also known as ‘mobile first’ design.  Plain and simple, this is a practice to make your website work on a phone, tablet and computer seamlessly without needing another website or additional work.

Why should you have a responsive website?  Simple, because it’s the right thing to do!  Major companies like Google, say you should.  Even more importantly, people are accessing websites on their phones and tablets more frequently, so you’ll want your website to look and function just as well as it does on a laptop of desktop computer.  Already in love with your websites current design?  We can retrofit responsive techniques onto your existing site and make it mobile friendly, while preserving the original look and feel.  Sites By Coop has been hand crafting responsive designed websites for close to 5 years now, let us help you today.
